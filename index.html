About NeuroAI

NeuroAI is your AI-powered mental health assistant, designed to provide personalized support, guidance, and resources for managing mental health. With advanced AI, it learns from your behavior, offers real-time feedback, and helps improve well-being.

Features

  • Symptom Checker
  • Predictive Diagnosis
  • Empathetic Response System
  • Real-time Mental Health Support
  • Cultural Sensitivity and Bias Prevention

Integration with Labs and Tools

NeuroAI integrates securely with diagnostic tools such as IoT devices, wearables, and connected platforms that collect health data like DNA results, glucose levels, hormone indicators, and vitals.

Using encrypted API connections, NeuroAI connects to healthcare and lab databases to pull in only essential biomarker data, such as stress markers, genetic predispositions, and meditation response indicators, while prioritizing user privacy and control.

How It Works

  1. User Onboarding: Voluntary, secure onboarding using minimal data.
  2. Data Integration: Connects to wearables and labs to gather relevant health signals.
  3. AI Biomarker Engine: Analyzes biomarker inputs to tailor personalized mental and physical wellness support.
  4. Accessible Storage: Health data is stored locally or in the cloud, controlled by the user.
